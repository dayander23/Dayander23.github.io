About Me

My name is Daylon F. Anderson Jr., and I am a 25-year-old graduate student at Old Dominion University, pursuing a Master’s in Computer Science. I am highly motivated, eager to learn new skills, and committed to advancing myself both academically and professionally. With a strong foundation in computer science and an unwavering drive to grow, I aim to contribute meaningfully to the field while continuously challenging myself to reach new heights.
